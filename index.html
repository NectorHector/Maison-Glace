La Philosophie

We do not make ice.
We compose it.

In the silence of the Karakoram, where air is thin and water runs untouched for centuries, we found what we were looking for. Not merely water, but the memory of water — distilled through millennia of stone and glacier, carrying within it a purity that cannot be manufactured.

L'Atelier

From Source to Sculpture

Every cube that leaves our atelier has been touched by seventeen hands, tested by three independent laboratories, and aged in conditions that mirror the silence of its origin.

01
Expedition & Collection
Our hydrologists journey to select tributaries above 4,500 metres, collecting water only during the brief alpine melt window between late spring and early summer.
02
Laboratory Verification
Each batch undergoes 847 individual tests across three independent facilities in Geneva, confirming mineral composition, isotopic age, and structural viability.
03
Directional Freezing
Over twenty-one days, the water is frozen in a single direction at precisely controlled temperatures, forcing impurities outward and yielding absolute optical clarity.
04
Hand Finishing
Master artisans sculpt each cube to specification using temperature-controlled tools. No two cubes are identical. Every edge is deliberate.
